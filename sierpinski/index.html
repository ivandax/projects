Sierpinski's Triangle

About the Experiment

The goal is to showcase how a seemlingly random process can produce a non-random outcome.

The Process

A die roll yields a random result from 1 to 6.
We assign a corner of an imaginary triangle to each pair of results, like this:

  • 1 or 2 => Corner A
  • 3 or 4 => Corner B
  • 5 or 6 => Corner C

Starting from center of the triangle, with each die roll, we will move midway towards the associated corner.

We draw a pixel in this spot, and from here, we will repeat the process with the new die roll, so on and so forth.

Hit the 'Run' button to see the magic happen! :)
